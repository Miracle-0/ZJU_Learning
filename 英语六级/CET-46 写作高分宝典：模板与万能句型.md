15. > # 四六级写作万能句型 + 模板（整理版）
    >
    > ## 一、开头段常用句型
    >
    > ### 引出现象/话题
    > - With the rapid development of society / economy / science and technology, **... has become a topic of heated discussion**.  
    >   随着社会/经济/科技的快速发展，……已成为热议话题。
    > - In recent years, **there has been a growing concern over...**  
    >   近年来，人们越来越关注……
    > - Nowadays, **it is commonly believed that...**  
    >   如今，人们普遍认为……
    > - **... plays an increasingly important role in our daily life.**  
    >   ……在我们的日常生活中扮演着越来越重要的角色。
    >
    > ### 提出观点/立场
    > - From my point of view, **... is of great significance.**  
    >   在我看来，……具有重要意义。
    > - As far as I am concerned, **... should be encouraged / taken seriously.**  
    >   就我而言，……应被鼓励/认真对待。
    > - I firmly believe that **... is essential to...**  
    >   我坚信……对……至关重要。
    >
    > ---
    >
    > ## 二、主体段常用句型
    >
    > ### 1. 列举原因 / 优势 / 影响
    > - There are several reasons accounting for this phenomenon.  
    >   这一现象有若干原因。
    > - First and foremost, ...  
    >   首先，……
    > - Secondly, ...  
    >   其次，……
    > - Last but not least, ...  
    >   最后但同样重要的是，……
    > - One major advantage of ... is that it can ...  
    >   ……的一大优势在于它能……
    > - It not only ..., but also ...  
    >   它不仅……，而且……
    >
    > ### 2. 表达对比 / 转折
    > - Admittedly, ... However, ...  
    >   诚然，……然而，……
    > - Some people argue that ..., while others believe that ...  
    >   一些人认为……，而另一些人则认为……
    > - Although ... may seem beneficial, it can also lead to ...  
    >   尽管……看似有益，但也可能导致……
    >
    > ### 3. 举例说明
    > - A typical example is ...  
    >   一个典型的例子是……
    > - Take ... as an example.  
    >   以……为例。
    > - For instance / For example, ...  
    >   例如，……
    >
    > ### 4. 强调重要性 / 必要性
    > - It is crucial / vital / imperative that ...  
    >   ……是至关重要的/必要的。
    > - Without ..., it would be impossible to ...  
    >   如果没有……，就不可能……
    > - We cannot overemphasize the importance of ...  
    >   我们再怎么强调……的重要性也不为过。
    >
    > ---
    >
    > ## 三、结尾段常用句型
    >
    > ### 1. 总结观点
    > - In conclusion / To sum up, ...  
    >   总之，……
    > - All in all, it is clear that ...  
    >   总的来说，很明显……
    > - From what has been discussed above, we can draw the conclusion that ...  
    >   从以上讨论可知，我们可以得出结论：……
    >
    > ### 2. 提出建议 / 呼吁行动
    > - Therefore, it is high time that we took effective measures to ...  
    >   因此，我们是时候采取有效措施来……
    > - Only in this way can we ...  
    >   只有这样，我们才能……
    > - Let’s take action now to make our world a better place!  
    >   让我们现在就行动起来，让世界变得更美好！
    >

    > ## 四、万能写作模板（按题型分类）
    >
    > ### 模板一：观点类议论文（支持/反对某观点）
    > ```markdown
    > **Title**: [Your Title]
    > 
    > Nowadays, there is a heated debate over whether **[topic]**. Some people argue that **[opposing view]**, while others believe that **[your view]**. As far as I am concerned, I strongly support the latter.
    > 
    > First of all, **[reason 1]**. For example, **[example]**. Secondly, **[reason 2]**. Moreover, **[reason 3, if needed]**.
    > 
    > In conclusion, **[restate your opinion]**. Only by doing so can we **[positive outcome]**.
    > ```
    >
    > ---
    >
    > ### 模板二：利弊分析类（Discuss advantages and disadvantages）
    > ```markdown
    > **Title**: The Pros and Cons of [Topic]
    > 
    > With the development of ..., **[topic]** has become increasingly common. Like everything else, it has both advantages and disadvantages.
    > 
    > On the one hand, **[advantage 1]**. In addition, **[advantage 2]**. On the other hand, **[disadvantage 1]**. What’s worse, **[disadvantage 2]**.
    > 
    > Overall, although **[topic]** brings some problems, its benefits outweigh its drawbacks **(or vice versa)**. Therefore, we should **[suggestion]**.
    > ```
    >
    > ---
    >
    > ### 模板三：问题解决类（Describe a problem and propose solutions）
    > ```markdown
    > **Title**: How to Solve the Problem of [Issue]
    > 
    > In recent years, **[problem]** has aroused widespread concern. It not only affects **[group/area]**, but also leads to **[negative consequences]**.
    > 
    > There are several possible solutions. First, the government should **[solution 1]**. Second, schools and families ought to **[solution 2]**. Finally, individuals can also contribute by **[solution 3]**.
    > 
    > Only through joint efforts can we effectively tackle this issue and build a better future.
    > ```
    >
    > ---
    >
    > ### 模板四：书信/邮件类（如建议信、申请信、感谢信等）
    > ```markdown
    > Dear [Sir/Madam / Name],
    > 
    > I am writing to **[purpose: express my gratitude / apply for ... / make a suggestion about ...]**.
    > 
    > [Body paragraph 1: Explain background or reason]  
    > [Body paragraph 2: Provide details / suggestions / requests]
    > 
    > I would appreciate it if you could **[action requested]**. Thank you for your time and consideration.
    > 
    > Yours sincerely,  
    > [Your Name]
    > ```
    >
    > > ✅ **常见书信开头结尾补充**：
    > > - 开头：I’m Li Hua, a student from... / I recently visited your...
    > > - 结尾：Looking forward to your reply. / I hope you will find my suggestions helpful.

    > ## 五、加分表达（提升语言档次）
    >
    > | 普通表达  | 升级表达                                      |
    > | --------- | --------------------------------------------- |
    > | important | of great significance / vital / indispensable |
    > | many      | a considerable number of / numerous           |
    > | think     | hold the view that / maintain that            |
    > | help      | contribute to / facilitate / promote          |
    > | bad       | detrimental / adverse / negative impact       |
    >
    > ---
    >
    > > 💡 **使用建议**：
    > > - 不必死记硬背全文，重点掌握**结构逻辑 + 关键句型**；
    > > - 写作时灵活替换关键词，避免模板化痕迹；
    > > - 注意衔接词（however, therefore, furthermore）的合理使用，增强连贯性。
